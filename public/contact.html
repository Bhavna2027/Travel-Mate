Frequently Asked Questions

Real answers to questions about safety verification, companion matching, and platform logistics.

How does TravelMate ensure traveler safety?
Every user who wants to request matches or message group members must verify their identity. We use secure integrations to verify Aadhaar cards and run an instant browser-side face liveness check. This ensures all travelers are real, verified individuals before they join any circle.
What happens if I trigger an SOS alert?
Triggering the SOS button instantly dispatches a safety alert containing your exact GPS coordinates to your saved emergency contacts. Simultaneously, our backend notifies active emergency helplines and alerts other members in your matching travel group, keeping everyone in the loop during a safety concern.
How is compatibility calculated for group matches?
We cluster open matching requests based on destination, overlapping travel dates, budget tiers (economy/mid/premium), and interests (e.g. trekking, food tours, leisure). Our database matches you with travelers who fit your preferences so there are no surprises.
Do you verify accommodation stays?
Yes. Stays listed in our Accommodations tab are vetted based on physical security audits, safety history, 24/7 front desk availability, security presence, and user reviews from female travelers.
How are expense splits and guides managed?
Once matches are locked, members share a cooperative group interface. You can log trip expenses on a shared ledger which automatically calculates optimal debt transfers. You can also book vetted local tour guides in safety-reviewed tourist zones directly.
