Essential Safety Guidelines

Follow these safety protocols to ensure safe and happy group travel adventures.

1. Verify your companion's trust badges
Before planning a detailed trip with any member, view their profile in the app and verify their KYC and trust score badges. Avoid traveling in groups where members decline to complete verification.
2. Activate live location sharing in remote areas
When exploring hiking trails or new city neighborhoods, toggle "Live Location Share" on this safety page. This updates your companion group with your coordinates automatically.
3. Meet in public tourist hubs first
When arriving at your destination, plan your first group match meeting at a public café, bus station, or main town square. Take some time to introduce yourselves before heading to accommodations.
4. Keep emergency contact information active
Ensure you have added at least one primary emergency contact on this safety desk. In an emergency, our SOS alerts will reach them instantly without requiring you to dial phone numbers.
5. Report suspicious users immediately
If a member behaves inappropriately, violates trust boundaries, or seems fraudulent, tap "File Abuse Report." Our Moderation Risk Engine auto-bans users who cross critical warning thresholds.
